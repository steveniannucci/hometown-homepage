Visit Wellington

Take some time away from the city life and enjoy a nice vacay in the countryside of Florida.

Top three activities to do at Wellington

Visit over 50 stores in one place

Enjoy shopping, dining at the food court, and entertainment at the cinema and at the arcade in The Mall at Wellington Green.

Attend free events by the community

Concerts, movie nights, and other local events are hosted at the Wellington Ampitheater, the town's outdoor community venue.

Watch equestrian sports live from the arena

Witness equestrian competiton year-round at Wellington International, the most recognizable equestrian sporting venue in the US.
